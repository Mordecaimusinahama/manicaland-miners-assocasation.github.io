manicaland miners association makes life better for us all

Manicaland Mining is a mining company based in Zimbabwe's Manicaland Province that aims to improve the quality of life of miners and their communities in the region. The company provides technical assistance, training, and financial support to local artisanal and small-scale miners,

  • Technical assistance
  • Access to finance and investment opportunities
  • Community development projects
